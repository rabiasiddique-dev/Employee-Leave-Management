Company Leave Policy

Welcome to LeaveFlow's company leave policy. This document outlines the types of leave available to employees, eligibility criteria, and the process for applying for leave.

General Leave Principles

  • All leave applications must be submitted through the LeaveFlow portal.
  • Leave requests should be submitted at least [X days/weeks] in advance, except in cases of emergency.
  • Approval of leave is subject to business requirements and manager discretion.
  • Unused eligible leave may be carried forward or encashed as per company policy.

Types of Leave

1. Annual Leave (Paid Leave)

Employees are entitled to [Number] days of annual leave per year, accrued убий (e.g., monthly).

Eligibility: All permanent employees after completion of probation period.

Application: Must be applied for at least [Y days] in advance.

2. Sick Leave (Paid Leave)

Employees are entitled to [Number] days of sick leave per year.

Eligibility: All employees from the date of joining.

Documentation: A medical certificate may be required for sick leave exceeding [Z days] consecutive days.

Notification: Employees should notify their manager as early as possible on the first day of sickness.

3. Casual Leave (Paid/Unpaid - Specify)

Employees may avail [Number] days of casual leave per year for unforeseen personal matters.

Note: [Specify if prior approval is needed or if it's for emergencies].

4. Maternity/Paternity Leave

Eligible employees are entitled to maternity/paternity leave as per statutory regulations and company policy. Please refer to the detailed [Maternity/Paternity Policy Document/HR] for more information.

5. Unpaid Leave

Unpaid leave may be granted under exceptional circumstances at the discretion of management. This must be discussed and approved in advance.

Leave Application and Approval Process

  1. Employee submits a leave request via the LeaveFlow portal.
  2. The request is routed to the employee's direct manager for approval.
  3. The manager reviews the request based on team workload and employee's leave balance.
  4. Employee is notified of the approval or rejection status via email and in-app notification.

This policy is subject to change. Employees will be notified of any updates. For any clarifications, please contact the HR department.
