Help & Support

If you encounter any issues while using the LeaveFlow system or have questions regarding leave policies, please refer to the resources below or contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Applying for Leave:

  • How do I apply for leave?
    Navigate to the "Apply Leave" section from your dashboard, fill in the required details (leave type, dates, reason), and submit the form.
  • Can I cancel a leave application?
    Yes, you can typically cancel a pending leave application from the "My Leaves" page. If the leave is already approved, please contact your manager or HR.
  • How can I check my leave balance?
    Your current leave balance is displayed on your main dashboard.

Technical Issues:

  • I forgot my password. What should I do?
    Please use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page to reset your password.
  • The system is not working as expected.
    Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or try a different browser. If the issue persists, please contact IT support.
